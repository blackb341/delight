ABOUT

Delight International School: A Beacon of Excellence and Innovation Nestled in the heart of a vibrant community, Delight International School stands as a shining example of educational excellence and innovation. With its commitment to nurturing well-rounded individuals and fostering a love for learning, the school has established itself as a leader in providing a world-class education. From the moment students step through its doors, they are greeted by an environment designed to inspire and motivate. The school's state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, cutting-edge laboratories, and expansive libraries, create a dynamic learning atmosphere where students can thrive. These resources, combined with a dedicated and passionate faculty, ensure that every child has the opportunity to reach their fullest potential. One of the most remarkable aspects of Delight International School is its emphasis on a holistic education.

Micheal Anele embarked on this journey with a clear mission: to provide a nurturing environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. Their dedication to fostering an inclusive, innovative, and globally-minded curriculum has set Delight International School apart. Under their leadership, the school has become a haven for students, parents, and educators alike, emphasizing a holistic approach to education that prepares students for a dynamic and interconnected world. What sets Micheal Anale apart is not just their vision, but their hands-on involvement in every aspect of the school’s development—from curriculum design to community engagement. Their unwavering commitment to quality education and student well-being is evident in the school’s success and the positive impact it has on its community.
